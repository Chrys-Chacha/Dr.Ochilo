CAREER ACHIEVEMENTS

2017 to date Senator Migori County.

2011 - 2015 Kenya Nuclear Electricity Board.
Chief Executive Officer & Executive Chairman of the Board of Directors

2017 -2018 CHAIR - AFCONE.
The Kenya Nuclear Electricity Board Executive Chairman, Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko was the chair of the African Commission on Nuclear Energy (AFCONE). This development during the ongoing fifth ordinary session of AFCONE at the African Union headquarters in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, was and is a boost to Kenya as it pursues the development of its nuclear power programme.

2010 – 2013 Kenya Water Institute.
Chair of Board of Directors

2009 – 2012 Lake Basin Development Authority.
Chair of implementation Committee of the Board of Directors

2008 – 2009 Southern Sudan.
Lead Consultant on Legal & Legislative Matters.
Advisor to the Cabinet.

2007 - 2011 Kajwang & Ayacko Advocates.

1997 – 2007 Elected Member of Parliament, Rongo Constituency.

2007 - Member of Public Accounts Committee.

2004 – 2005:- Minister of Gender, Sports, Culture and Social Services:
i) Established the Gender and Development Commission and later the Gender Department
ii) Established the Disability Council
iii) Hosted the first ever World Cross country championships
iv) Procured parliament’s approval of the Youth and Sports policies.

2003 – 2004 - Minister of Energy.
Led the Ministry’s reform programme which resulted in:
i) Reinstatement of the Energy sector reform programme funded by the World Bank and other development partners i.e. AFD, JICA etc.
ii) Privatization of KPLC and its subsequent profitability
iii) Government’s approval of extension of pipeline to Uganda
iv) Restructuring of State Corporation in the Energy sector and their subsequent profitability.

1999 – 2001: Chairman of Public Investments Committee.
i) Led the Committee in auditing various state corporations and procured parliament’s unanimous adoption of various PIC reports.
ii) Team and consensus builder in the Committee

1998 – 2002 Public Investment Committee.
