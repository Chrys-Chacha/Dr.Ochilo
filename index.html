TRACK RECORD AS MP AND MINISTER

Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko as an MP for Rongo constituency left an indelible development mark which phenomenally improved lives despite serving as an MP without CDF until 2004 when CDF was enacted by an act of parliament which gave him only one year with a CDF kitty. However, through his immense love for development Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko left behind a phenomenal record that is verifiable. Before Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko became MP, only the following Schools existed:
Manyatta, Ulanda, Dede, Pe Hill, Owiro Akoko, Kokuro, Kanga, Kanyawanga, Kangeso, Kodero Obara and Oyugi Ogango.

As soon as Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko took over he quickly initiated the construction of day secondary schools to bring cheaper, accessible and affordable education to his constituents. Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko built to completion the following schools:
1. West Sakwa_ Agongo Secondary, Rabondo Secondary, Gamba Secondary, Kamiyawa Secondary, Siruti Secondary and Kwe mixed.
2. North Sakwa- Kuoyo Kodalo, Kanyasrega Nyokal, Yago Secondary and Nyakuru Secondary.
3. Central Sakwa- Komolo rume Secondary, Got Oguamrondo Secondary, Awendo polytechnic.
4. East Sakwa- Rinya mixed, St. Marks Tom Mboya Secondary, Kogelo mixed and Nyasori polytechnic.
5. South Sakwa- Malunga Secondary and Ngo'onga Secondary.
6. South Kamagambo- St. Patrick Kamgundho Secondary, Lang' Arek Secondary, Arundo and Omware.
7. East Kamagambo - Nyamuga Secondary, Nyangao, Opapo mixed and Apondo mixed.
8. Central Kamagambo - Nyarach Mixed and Winyo Mixed.
9. West Kamagambo - Rakwaro mixed sec and Sigiria mixed.
10. North Kamagambo - Kameji mixed Secondary, Minyenya mixed Secondary, Kadianga mixed Secondary and Ofuang'a mixed.
In a bid to improve medicare services and promote affordable and accessible healthcare to his constituents, Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko constructed:
a) West Sakwa - Rabondo health center and Ringa Kodongo health centre.
b) Central Sakwa - Ombo Bita health facility, Otacho, Kokuro health centre. Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko Sanctioned the upgrading of Awendo Health centre to Awendo Level 4 and a Doctor was posted.
c) East Sakwa- Bonde Health Centre and Angaga health center 4. South Sakwa- Built two hospital Wards at Mariwa, Nyakuru health Centre, Kwoyo health Centre and Angogo health Centre.
d) Central Kamagambo, Kodero Bara dispensary, Upgrading of Rongo health centre to Rongo Level 4.
e) South Kamagambo - Kitere health centre, Lang'arek and Kamgundo.
f) West Kamagambo- Kangeso, Chamgi wadu, East Kamagambo- Opapo, Ngodhe and Miyare.
g) North Kamagambo- Lwala, Tonye and Ofwanga.
In a bid to stop the recurrent border clashes between Kisii and Luo, Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko built police posts alongside the border which forthrightly stopped the clashes and has never been witnessed ever again. As an astute legislator in the history of the National Assembly, Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko was elected the YOUNGEST PIC chair in the assembly a position he served diligently to completion of his term. Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko sponsored several bills to parliament amongst which are:
1. Sugar Act of 2001 sponsored by Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko and debated through stages until its adoption.
2. Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko Seconded the road act.
3. Parliamentary service act.
4. CDF Act, Dr. Ochilo G.M. Ayacko was a proponent of the CDF act which he seconded on the floor of the house. Every resident of the larger Rongo, will remember that being a bold and a respected leader of character, Dr. Ochilo G.M Ayacko chagrined the establishment of Rongo and Awendo districts under one Constituency with respect to their multi ethnic diversity.
